Privacy & Security Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

At Ventora AI, we prioritize your privacy and security. This policy explains how we handle your information in our web-based Medical Information Assistant (MIA), designed for educational purposes.

Ventora AI runs primarily in your browser, keeping most data local on your device. We collect no personal information unless you provide it voluntarily, and we do not require accounts or logins.

1. Data Collected and Stored Locally

  • All chats, conversation history, settings, attached files, prescriptions, and diet plans are stored exclusively in your browser’s localStorage or IndexedDB.
  • We do not transmit this data to our servers or any third party.
  • Your information stays on your device and is deleted if you clear browser data or use the “Clear All Data” feature.
  • You control and own your data fully.

2. Data Sent to Third-Party APIs

Ventora AI sends certain data temporarily to external services for functionality:

  • Chat Responses: We forward your queries and conversation context to the Groq API (or similar LLM providers) to generate AI responses. The provider processes the data only for the response and does not store it long-term. (Groq applies zero data retention by default for inference requests.)
  • File Text Extraction (OCR): When you upload images or PDFs for text extraction, we use the https://ocr.space/OCRAPI. Uploaded files and extracted text are deleted immediately after processing—the service stores nothing.

These transmissions occur only when you initiate the feature; no background data sharing happens. We select providers with strong privacy practices that avoid storing your content.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

  • We employ no cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.
  • We log no IP addresses, usage patterns, or personal identifiers.
  • The app functions fully offline-capable except for AI response and OCR features.

4. Security Measures

  • All local data stays on your device, protected by your browser’s security.
  • Communications with APIs use HTTPS encryption.
  • As an open-source project (where applicable), community members can review the code for transparency.

5. Children’s Privacy

Ventora AI does not target children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided information, contact us to request deletion.

6. Medical Disclaimer

Ventora AI serves as an educational tool only. It does not offer medical advice, diagnoses, treatments, or prescriptions. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for health concerns. We bear no liability for misuse or reliance on the information provided.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify significant changes within the app or on our site.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this policy or your data, email: support@ventora.ai

Thank you for trusting Ventora AI. We are committed to maintaining the highest privacy and security standards.
